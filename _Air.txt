Aircraft General

    Thrust:  Apply ThrustForce forward, and a % of that to cancel out sideways momentum
    Break:   Apply BreakForce opposite travel direction.
    Ascend:  Apply LiftForce upwards
    Descend: TBD

    LiftForce: Amount of force that can be applied up or sideways in normal flight
    RotationForce: Amount of force that can be applied for rotation during sliding

Aircraft Fly Tick

    Canceling?
        Decelerate

    Climb? If target is above Craft
        Increase delta Z until MaxAltitude

    Decend? If target is below Craft, and
        Decrease delta Z

    Decelerate? If we are close enough, with no further waypoints
        Apply Breaks

    Accelerate? If Facing the target
        Apply Thrust

    Else: Turn?
        Should Slide?
            Apply Thrust

        No Slide?
            Apply Lift perpendicular to travel direction
