
We have a nuclear silo and it fires a nuke. The nuke is much more powerful than in the original red alert, which goes along with the goal of this mod. However, a real nuke would probably blow up the whole map, so here is the rationale:

- The nuke we have now represents a tactical nuke, one that might be used on a battlefield in a non-MAD-scenario, where a side uses it tactically and militarily. 

- Nukes are not really meant to be used in the game, they are part of a gimmicky "Doomsday" nuclear armageddon theme of this mod. As many fear, a thirld world war would likely mean the end of humanity through nuclear annihilation. I want the usage of nukes in this mod to somewhat trigger that feeling, more than the feeling "Hell yeah lets fucking nuke them!" or maybe both, that is up to the player, but that is what I would like to "feel" as a player when nukes start exploding. 

- So I imagine that, instead of having time limited games that simply end after a certain time, there could be a some kind of DEFCON system, where the game starts at DEFCON 3, and when it reaches DEFCON 1 each player has the option to nuke the opponent, if either of them is about to lose they can freely use it, ending the game for both players, declaring the one who ordered the all out nuclear attack the loser.

- We could also allow for single small tactical nuclear launches at defcon 2 or early in defcon 1, meant for the already losing player to use to regain the upper hand, and if they manage to win after using a nuke, it still counts as a win, but when the other player has used a nuke first ...
