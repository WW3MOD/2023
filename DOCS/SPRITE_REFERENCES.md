# Sprite & Asset References from Other Mods

Sources of sprites, models, and assets that could be borrowed or adapted for WW3MOD. These are OpenRA mods with potentially compatible art.

---

## Combined Arms
https://www.moddb.com/mods/command-conquer-combined-arms/images/showcase-soviet#imagebox

### Buildings
- **HQ / EYE** -- Bunker with rotating sat-dish (commcenter), advanced version
- **REP** -- Repair building, possible replacement for FIX
- **UPGC** -- Generals command center (upgradeable, or use as static)
- **nodfact** -- Cooler than RA original factory (needs scorpion image removed)

### Defenses
- **ATWR** -- Advanced guard tower (looks more like air control tower)
- **HTUR** -- Grand cannon, coastal defense style
- **STWR** -- Sonic tower (looks like rotating radar)
- **LTUR** -- Laser turret (potential anti-UAV)

### Infantry
- **U3** -- G.I., deployable into bunker
- **RMBO** -- Commando, red beret
- **SEAL** -- Same but green beret
- **BORI** -- Boris, could work as officer unit
- **SAB** -- Like Boris with black uniform
- **E8** -- Desolator, RPG-ish look; e8d (deployed) could work as reload animation
- **XO** -- Powersuit, very futuristic (for potential futuristic mode)

### Vehicles
- **CHPR** -- Command vehicle chassis (swap turret for radar)
- **CRYO** -- MLRS with rotatable turret (HIMARS/M270 lookalike)
- **BGGY** -- Buggy scout vehicle
- **HMMV** -- Humvee, also TOW version
- **LTNK** -- Light tank, good model
- **MSAM** -- SAM vehicle, looks good
- **MLRS** -- Two missiles, Soviet looking
- **IFV** -- BTR chassis with turret
- **TNKD** -- Tank destroyer, good generic tank
- **TTRK** -- Supply truck
- **WTNK** -- Microwave tank (prototype? could cause panic in non-veterans)
- **BATF** -- Battle fortress (uses loaded condition for different units)
- **APOC** -- Apocalypse tank (chassis + different turret for realism)

### Aircraft
- **HORN** -- Hornet
- **UAV** -- Drone
- **NHAW** -- Nighthawk naval helicopter
- **SUK** -- Sukhoi (futuristic delta wing)
- **B2B** -- B2 bomber
- **SMIG** -- Perfect for Sukhoi T-50
- **C17** -- Transport plane
- **RAH** -- RAH-66 Comanche helicopter
- **HARR** -- Harrier
- **AURO** -- Aurora bomber (fictional but realistic)
- **MIG** -- Different from original RA MIG

### Naval
- **SB** -- Quick boat
- **CV** -- Drone carrier / cruise missile launcher
- **DD2** -- Frigate (cannon + missile launcher)
- **PT2** -- Patrol boat (smaller DD2)

### Also has
- **Capturable@DRIVER_DEAD** trait -- interesting for disabled vehicle capture
